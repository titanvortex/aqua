AQUAMARINE FLEET [AQUA]

--- Fleet Overview ---
Welcome, Captain! Our fleet is open to everyone without strict rules or quotas, and anyone can join casually for fleet bonuses. Due to this nature, many of our fleet members are beginners, and we have a diverse community with members from various languages and nationalities. Let's all enjoy the game together!

--- Exclusion Guidelines ---
If you remain offline for the following periods, you may be removed from the fleet:
・Players with contributions of less than 1000 oil < 1 week >
・Players with contributions of 1000 or more oil < 1 month >
You may be removed if the following actions are confirmed:
・Acts that disturb other players or go against the spirit of fair play
・Actions that violate laws or regulations

--- 除名ガイドライン ---
以下の期間非オンライン状態が続くと艦隊から除名される事があります。
・石油の貢献が1000未満のプレイヤー < 1週間 >
・石油の貢献が1000以上のプレイヤー < 1ヶ月間 >
以下の行為が確認された場合は除名される事があります。
・他のプレイヤーに対する迷惑行為やフェアプレイの精神に反する行為
・法や規約に抵触する行為
